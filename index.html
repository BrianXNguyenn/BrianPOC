Keyboard Navigation Guide

  • Tab - Move to next link or button
  • Shift + Tab - Move to previous link or button
  • Enter - Open link or activate button
  • ↑ Up Arrow - Move to previous article in a list
  • ↓ Down Arrow - Move to next article in a list

Note: Arrow keys work when navigating through article links, making it easier to read through content without using a mouse.
